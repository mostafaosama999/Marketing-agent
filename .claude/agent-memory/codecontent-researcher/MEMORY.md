## High-Value Sources

- [Wellfound](https://wellfound.com) — Early-stage startup job listings. Reliability: 4/5
- [G2](https://g2.com) — Product reviews mentioning pain points. Reliability: 4/5
- [RevOpsCareers](https://revopscareers.com) — Startup marketing/sales job postings with full JDs. Reliability: 3/5

## Search Query Patterns That Worked
- `[company] funding crunchbase [year]` → fast funding data
- `"[company]" G2 reviews OR Glassdoor reviews [year]` → review signals
- `[company] careers jobs [year]` → current openings across boards
- `"[company]" "write for us" OR "guest post" OR "community writing"` → CWP discovery
- `site:linkedin.com/company "[company]"` + separate CEO name search → leadership data

## ICP Patterns Observed
- Companies with DevGTM Academy / educational content hubs = content investment signal but not a CWP
- B2D companies with product marketing JDs citing "long-form blog posts and research-backed case studies" = outsourcing candidate (content need explicit, team lean)
- Pre-Series A/early Seed companies based in India/Bangalore with US HQ often have thin content teams despite active blogs — reliance on founders writing
